About

I'm Janet, an experienced Product Manager and UX Designer who starts from the user and turns complexity into something simple, clear, and detailed enough to ship. Across 9+ years in Travel Tech and HCM SaaS, I've shipped 20+ features for platforms serving thousands of merchants worldwide.

Not classically design-trained — I came from English literature, and that's my edge. I untangle messy, multi-stakeholder problems, sweat the details others skip, and turn them into flows that just make sense. I read API docs end-to-end and brief engineering myself — so my work moves real numbers: conversion, cost, adoption, satisfaction.
